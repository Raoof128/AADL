# Code of Conduct

We are committed to a welcoming, harassment-free community. All contributors must follow the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

## Standards
- Use inclusive and respectful language.
- Assume good intent; seek clarification before escalating.
- Focus on constructive feedback and collaboration.

## Enforcement
- Report violations to the maintainers at `security@example.local`.
- Maintainers will review and respond promptly, maintaining confidentiality.
- Repeated or severe violations may result in loss of contribution privileges.

By participating, you agree to uphold this code of conduct.
